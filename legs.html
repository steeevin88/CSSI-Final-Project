Leg Press

Tutorial: https://youtu.be/VFk3RzndUEc

Suggestions:

  1. 2–3 sets of 8–15 repetitions
  2. Choose a weight that allows you to maintain good technique throughout all sets and repetitions

Muscles worked: Quadriceps, Glutes, Adductors

Leg Extensions

Tutorial: https://youtu.be/IqUhWKkpub8

Suggestions:

  1. 3 sets of 8–12 repetitions
  2. Choose a moderate weight

Muscles worked: Quadriceps

Leg Curls (lying/seated)

Tutorials:

Seated: https://youtu.be/oFxEDkppbSQ
Lying: https://youtu.be/vl5nUdE9mWM

Suggested Reps:

  1. Beginner: 3-4 sets of 12-18 reps using a light weight
  2. Advanced: 3-4 sets of 10-15 reps using a heavier weight

Muscles worked: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Calves

Hack Squats

Tutorial: https://youtu.be/zN_JJoUyI3M

Suggested Reps:

  1. 3-4 sets of 10-12 reps
  2. Weight recommendation: 65-70% of your 1 rep max

Muscles worked: Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings

Calf Raises (standing/seated)

Tutorials:

Seated: https://youtu.be/y2ueC0LggrI
Standing: https://youtu.be/GAQ-oohMhog

Suggested Reps:

  1. Start with 2 sets of 10 to 15 reps
  2. Rest for 30 to 60 seconds between sets

Muscles worked: Calves

Back Squats

Suggested Reps:

  1. 3-4 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Weight recommendation: 65-70% of your 1 rep max

Hip Adductors
