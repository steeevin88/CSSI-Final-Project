Bench Press

Tutorial: https://youtu.be/vcBig73ojpE

Suggestions:

  1. 3-5 sets of 8–12 repetitions
  2. Begin with lighter weight to loosen muscles and prevent injury
  3. Increment weight to increase difficulty and muscle strain

Muscles worked: Pectoralis, Deltoids, Serratus, Triceps, Biceps

Chest Press Machine

Tutorial: https://youtu.be/eozdVDA78K0

Suggestions:

  1. Set both sides at the same weight
  2. 3-5 sets of 10-12 repetitions with a comfortable weight or until failure
  3. Maintain consistent speed and extend arm for maximum strain

Muscles worked: Shoulders, Pectoral Muscles

Cable Flys

Tutorials:

Seated: https://youtu.be/Iwe6AmxVf7o

Suggested Reps:

  1. 3-4 sets of 10-12 reps using a comfortable weight
  2. Different height elevations strains different parts of the chest

Muscles worked: Triceps, Biceps, Shoulders, and Chest

Tutorial: https://youtu.be/eozdVDA78K0

Suggestions:

Muscles worked: Triceps, Shoulders, and Chest
